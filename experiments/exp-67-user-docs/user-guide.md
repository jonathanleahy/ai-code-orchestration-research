# Freelancer CRM User Guide

Welcome to your Freelancer CRM! This guide will help you learn how to use this tool to manage your clients, track your work, and handle invoices - all in one place.

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account
*(Note: This CRM doesn't have a login system yet, so you can start using it right away!)*

### Adding Your First Client
Let's get started by adding your first client. This is like making a new contact in your phone book, but for your business.

1. Click on the "Clients" tab at the top
2. Look for the green "Add New Client" button
3. Fill in their name, email, and phone number
4. Click "Save"
5. You're done! Your client is now in your system

### Your Dashboard
Your dashboard is like your main control room. It shows you:
- Quick stats about your clients
- Recent activities
- Upcoming deadlines
- Recent invoices
- Quick actions you can take

## Managing Clients

### Adding a Client
Adding a new client is simple:
1. Go to the "Clients" section
2. Click "Add New Client"
3. Enter their name, email, phone number, and any other details
4. Click "Save"
5. Your client is now saved in your system

### Editing Client Details
Sometimes you need to update your client's information:
1. Go to "Clients" section
2. Find your client in the list
3. Click the "Edit" button (usually a pencil icon)
4. Make your changes
5. Click "Save"

### Deleting a Client
If you need to remove a client (maybe they're no longer working with you):
1. Go to "Clients" section
2. Find the client you want to delete
3. Click the "Delete" button (usually a trash can icon)
4. Confirm you really want to delete them

### Searching Clients
Finding a specific client quickly:
1. Go to "Clients" section
2. Use the search box at the top
3. Type their name or email
4. The system will show only matching clients

## Activity Tracking

### Logging a Call
Keep track of all your client communications:
1. Go to "Activity" section
2. Click "Log New Activity"
3. Choose "Call" from the activity type
4. Select which client you called
5. Add details about what you talked about
6. Click "Save"

### Logging an Email
Record your email communications:
1. Go to "Activity" section
2. Click "Log New Activity"
3. Choose "Email" from the activity type
4. Select your client
5. Add subject line and summary
6. Click "Save"

### Viewing Activity History
See all your past activities with a client:
1. Go to "Clients" section
2. Select a client
3. Click on the "Activity" tab
4. You'll see all calls, emails, meetings, and notes in chronological order

## Invoicing

### Creating an Invoice
Make and send invoices to your clients:
1. Go to "Invoices" section
2. Click "Create New Invoice"
3. Select your client
4. Add invoice details like date and due date
5. Click "Save"

### Adding Line Items
Add the work you did to your invoice:
1. Open your invoice
2. Click "Add Line Item"
3. Enter description of work done
4. Add quantity and price
5. Click "Save"
6. Repeat for each item

### Sending an Invoice
Send your invoice to the client:
1. Open your invoice
2. Click "Send Invoice"
3. Choose how you want to send it (email, PDF, etc.)
4. Click "Send"
5. The client will receive it automatically

### Recording Payment
Mark when you've been paid:
1. Open your invoice
2. Click "Record Payment"
3. Enter amount received
4. Add payment date
5. Click "Save"
6. The system will show it as paid

### Voiding an Invoice
Cancel an invoice you made by mistake:
1. Open the invoice you want to void
2. Click "Void Invoice"
3. Confirm you want to void it
4. The invoice will be marked as void and won't be sent to client

### Printing an Invoice
Get a paper copy of your invoice:
1. Open the invoice
2. Click "Print Invoice"
3. Your computer will show a print preview
4. Click "Print" to print it

## FAQ

**1. What is a CRM?**
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. It's like a digital filing cabinet that helps you keep track of your clients, their communication history, and your work with them.

**2. Do I have to add all my clients at once?**
No! You can add clients one at a time as you meet them. There's no rush to add everyone at once.

**3. Can I see all my activities in one place?**
Yes! All your calls, emails, meetings, and notes are stored together in the Activity section so you can see your complete communication history with any client.

**4. What happens if I make a mistake on an invoice?**
You can edit invoices before sending them. If you already sent an invoice, you can void it and create a new one instead.

**5. How do I know if a client has paid me?**
When you record a payment, the system will show the invoice as "Paid." You can also see all payment history in the invoice details.

**6. Can I add notes about my clients?**
Yes! You can add notes about clients in the activity section or in their profile. This helps you remember important details about each client.

**7. What if I need to find a client quickly?**
Use the search bar at the top of the Clients section. Type part of their name or email and it will show only matching clients.

**8. How often should I update my client information?**
Update your client information whenever you get new contact details or when you have a new project with them. It's good practice to keep information current.

**9. Can I see how much money I've earned from each client?**
Yes! In your client profile, you can see all their invoices and payments. The system will show you their total amount owed or paid.

**10. What if I want to delete a client?**
You can delete a client if you no longer work with them, but be careful! Once deleted, the client and all their information will be permanently removed from the system.
