# Video Script

### Scene 1 (0-10s): Hook
- Visual: Fast-paced montage of a freelancer juggling multiple tasks - checking email, switching between apps, looking stressed
- Voiceover: "Tired of juggling clients, invoices, and deadlines? You're not alone."
- Music: Upbeat, slightly urgent instrumental

**Voiceover direction:** Fast pace, slightly urgent tone, emphasize "juggling" and "you're not alone"

### Scene 2 (10-25s): Problem
- Visual: Split screen showing a freelancer's messy desktop with scattered files, phone notifications, and a confused expression
- Voiceover: "Lost client details? Forgotten invoice due dates? Spending more time managing than creating? Freelancers waste 15+ hours weekly on admin tasks."
- Music: Building tension with subtle piano notes

**Voiceover direction:** Serious but hopeful tone, emphasize "15+ hours" and "admin tasks" with slight emphasis

### Scene 3 (25-45s): Solution (show the product)
- Visual: Clean, modern CRM interface showing client dashboard, activity timeline, and invoice creation
- Voiceover: "Simple CRM solves this with one powerful solution. Track client interactions, automate invoice reminders, and organize everything in one place. $15/month for unlimited clients."
- Music: Uplifting, confident melody

**Voiceover direction:** Confident, clear tone, emphasize "Simple CRM" and "$15/month" with strong emphasis

### Scene 4 (45-55s): Social proof
- Visual: Quick testimonials with freelancer photos and ratings
- Voiceover: "Join 2,000+ freelancers who save 10+ hours weekly. 'Finally, a tool that works with my workflow, not against it!' - Sarah M."
- Music: Warm, encouraging background

**Voiceover direction:** Friendly, authentic tone, emphasize "2,000+" and "10+ hours" with enthusiasm

### Scene 5 (55-60s): CTA
- Visual: Simple website URL with "simplecrm.com" and pricing
- Voiceover: "Start your free 14-day trial today. Simple CRM - $15/month for freelancers who want to focus on what they do best."
- Music: Final uplifting chord

**Voiceover direction:** Clear, decisive tone, emphasize "14-day trial" and "focus on what they do best"
