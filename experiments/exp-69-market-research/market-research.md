## Risk Assessment

### Market Risks
- **Saturated Market**: Time tracking is a crowded space, but universal integration is a differentiator
- **Declining Growth**: Time tracking tools are mature, but automation and cross-platform integration are emerging trends

### Technical Risks
- **API Complexity**: Integrating with 10+ PM tools can be technically challenging
- **Data Consistency**: Ensuring accurate time tracking across platforms
- **Scalability**: Handling high-volume data sync and reporting

### Competitive Risks
- **Big Players**: Asana, Monday.com, and Notion may introduce time tracking features
- **Existing Players**: Competitors like Harvest and Toggl could add universal integrations
- **User Switching**: Risk of users switching to competitors with better features
