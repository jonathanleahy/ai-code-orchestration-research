# Customer (Client-Facing)

**1. Does this CRM serve YOUR needs? (YES/PARTIALLY/NO)**
PARTIALLY

**2. What's the #1 thing you need that's MISSING?**
The ability to download invoices as PDFs. I need to send them to clients via email and keep them for my records, but I can't find a way to download them as proper PDFs. This is a dealbreaker for me.

**3. What feature would you use MOST?**
Simple invoicing - creating, sending, and tracking payments. I'm a freelancer who bills clients monthly, so I need to create invoices quickly and see payment status at a glance.

**4. What feature is USELESS to you?**
Activity log. I don't need to track calls, emails, or meetings. I'm a solo freelancer who just needs to know when I billed someone and when I got paid.

**5. Would you recommend this to a colleague at your level? Why/why not?**
Maybe, but only if they're just starting out. It's too basic for my needs, and the missing PDF download feature makes it unusable for my workflow. My colleague would be better served by something more robust that handles PDF generation and proper invoicing.

**6. Rate 1-10 for YOUR use case.**
3/10

The interface is clean and simple, which I appreciate, but it's missing the core functionality I actually need - PDF generation and proper invoice management. I can see this being useful for someone who just needs basic client management, but I need more professional features for my freelance business.
