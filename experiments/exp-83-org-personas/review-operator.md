# Operator (Day-to-Day)

**1. Does this CRM serve YOUR needs? (YES/PARTIALLY/NO)**

**PARTIALLY** - It's missing the core functionality I need for daily operations. I can't see cross-client views or get a comprehensive overview of all my work. The dashboard is useless without the ability to filter and sort across all clients.

**2. What's the #1 thing you need that's MISSING?**

**Cross-client activity feed with filtering** - I need to see all client activities in one place, sorted by date, with filters for type (calls, emails, meetings, notes) and time range. Right now I have to check each client individually to get the full picture of what's happening.

**3. What feature would you use MOST?**

**Search everything** - I literally use search 20+ times per day. I need to find specific client details, past communications, invoice numbers, or notes instantly. The current search is limited to clients only, which is useless.

**4. What feature is USELESS to you?**

**Simple invoicing** - I don't use the invoicing feature at all. I use QuickBooks for invoicing and just use the CRM for client management. The CRM's invoicing is clunky and doesn't integrate with my existing workflow.

**5. Would you recommend this to a colleague at your level? Why/why not?**

**NO** - It's missing the fundamental features I need to be productive. I'd recommend it to someone who just needs basic client storage, but not to someone who needs to manage multiple clients efficiently. It's like having a car without a steering wheel - it's a basic tool but not actually useful for real work.

**6. Rate 1-10 for YOUR use case.**

**3/10** - It's a decent starting point but completely inadequate for my daily needs. I'd need to add 5-6 other tools to make it work, which defeats the purpose of having a CRM in the first place.
