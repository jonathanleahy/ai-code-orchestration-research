# Owner (Business)

**1. Does this CRM serve YOUR needs? (YES/PARTIALLY/NO)**

**PARTIALLY**

It's a decent starting point, but it's missing the strategic business intelligence I need to run this operation effectively. I can manage clients and invoices, but I'm drowning in data without the insights to make informed decisions.

**2. What's the #1 thing you need that's MISSING?**

**Revenue forecasting and client lifetime value analytics**

I need to know not just who my top clients are, but *how much they'll contribute over time* and *when they might leave*. The current CRM shows me who I've billed, but gives me zero visibility into revenue trends, client retention patterns, or cash flow projections. I'm essentially flying blind on my business trajectory.

**3. What feature would you use MOST?**

**The dashboard with monthly P&L and cash flow forecast**

I'd use this daily. I need to see my monthly revenue trends, expenses, and cash flow position at a glance. The current dashboard is basic but would be invaluable if it could show:
- Month-over-month revenue growth
- Cash flow projections for next 3 months
- Pending vs. paid invoice breakdown
- Key performance indicators (revenue per client, avg invoice size, etc.)

**4. What feature is USELESS to you?**

**Activity log**

I don't need to track every call, email, or meeting. I'm a freelancer, not a sales machine. The activity log is just another data entry burden that doesn't inform my business decisions. I'd rather spend that time on actual client work than logging activities I don't need to track.

**5. Would you recommend this to a colleague at your level? Why/why not?**

**No, not yet.**

It might work for someone just starting out, but for someone who needs strategic business insights, it's too basic. I'd recommend it only if they're planning to outsource their business management or don't need the strategic view. It's missing the business intelligence that separates a good CRM from a useful one for business owners.

**6. Rate 1-10 for YOUR use case.**

**4/10**

It's functional but incomplete. It handles the basics of client management and invoicing, but I need something that actually helps me *run the business*, not just *track clients*. I need to see the big picture, not just the details.
