# Product Brief

## Product Brief: TimeSync

### Problem (2 sentences)  
Freelancers and small businesses struggle with fragmented time tracking across multiple project management tools, leading to inefficiency, inaccurate billing, and lost productivity. Current solutions require manual input or limited integrations, creating friction in workflow and data consistency.

### Solution (2 sentences)  
TimeSync is a universal time tracking system that seamlessly integrates with all major PM tools—Asana, Notion, Jira, Monday.com—automatically capturing time without manual effort. It offers real-time sync, unified reporting, and intelligent insights to streamline billing and project management.

### Target Customer  
Freelancers, contractors, and small business owners who use multiple project management platforms and need accurate, automated time tracking for invoicing and productivity.

### Key Features (5 bullet points)  
- Universal API-based integration with Asana, Notion, Jira, Monday.com and more  
- Automatic time tracking with no manual input required  
- Unified dashboard for cross-platform time reporting and insights  
- Smart alerts for billing discrepancies and productivity gaps  
- Freemium model with paid tiers for advanced analytics and integrations  

### Revenue Model  
Freemium tier for basic tracking; paid plans at $9–$29/month for advanced reporting, additional integrations, and team collaboration features.

### Success Metrics  
- Monthly Active Users (MAU) growth  
- User retention rate after 30 days  
- Number of integrated PM tools  
- Average time saved per user (measured via surveys)  
- Revenue per user (ARPU)  

### One-Line Brief (for the build pipeline)  
A universal time tracking system that integrates across all major project management tools to automate time logging and reporting for freelancers and small businesses.
