## Issue 1: No Data Subject Request Mechanism
**ISSUE:** No process or interface exists for clients to submit data subject requests (access, rectification, erasure, portability) as required by GDPR Article 15-21
**SEVERITY:** Critical
**UNIQUE:** YES

## Issue 2: No Data Processing Agreement (DPA) Framework
**ISSUE:** No mechanism for clients to consent to data processing or sign DPAs, required for GDPR compliance when processing personal data on behalf of clients
**SEVERITY:** Critical
**UNIQUE:** YES

**ISSUE:** No defined data retention periods or automatic deletion mechanisms for client data, violating GDPR Article 5(1)(e) requirement for data minimization and storage limitation
**SEVERITY:** Major
**UNIQUE:** YES
