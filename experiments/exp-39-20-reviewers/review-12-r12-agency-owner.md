## Issue 1: No Team Collaboration Features
**ISSUE:** No mechanism for multiple freelancers to collaborate on clients/projects or assign tasks/ownership
**SEVERITY:** Critical
**UNIQUE:** YES

As an agency owner managing 10 people with 200 clients, this is a fundamental flaw. You need to know who's working on what, assign projects, share notes, and track work distribution across your team. The current spec assumes single-user operation.

## Issue 2: No Project Management Capabilities
**ISSUE:** No project tracking, milestones, or task management features
**SEVERITY:** Critical
**UNIQUE:** YES

Your agency likely manages multiple concurrent projects per client. Without project management features, you can't track deliverables, deadlines, or work progress. You'd need to build this functionality separately or switch to a different system entirely.

## Issue 3: No Custom Fields or Client Segmentation
**ISSUE:** No ability to add custom client attributes or categorize clients by industry, budget, or service type
**SEVERITY:** Major
**UNIQUE:** YES

With 200 clients, you need to segment and filter efficiently. You'll want to track client types (e.g., "enterprise", "startup", "non-profit"), pricing tiers, service categories, or custom business attributes that aren't covered by basic contact info.

## Issue 4: No Reporting for Agency Metrics
**ISSUE:** Basic reporting doesn't include revenue tracking, team performance, or client retention metrics
**SEVERITY:** Major
**UNIQUE:** YES

As an agency owner, you need to track team productivity, revenue by client/project, billing rates, and performance metrics. The current "basic reporting" is insufficient for agency operations.

## Issue 5: No Multi-Client Access Control
**ISSUE:** No role-based access control to limit which team members can see which clients
**SEVERITY:** Major
**UNIQUE:** YES

In a 10-person agency, you need to control client access for confidentiality and compliance reasons. Not everyone should be able to see all clients or sensitive information.

## Issue 6: No Integration with Existing Tools
**ISSUE:** No mention of integrations with email, calendar, or payment platforms
**SEVERITY:** Minor
**UNIQUE:** YES

Your team likely uses Slack, Google Workspace, QuickBooks, or other tools. Without integrations, you'll have a fragmented workflow and manual data entry.

## Issue 7: No Mobile Access
**ISSUE:** No mention of mobile responsiveness or mobile app
**SEVERITY:** Minor
**UNIQUE:** YES

Your team needs to access client info and invoices on the go. Mobile access is essential for modern agency operations.
