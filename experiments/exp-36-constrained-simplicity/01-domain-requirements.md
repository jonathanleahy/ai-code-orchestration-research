## CLIENT MANAGEMENT

**Add Client** - Add new client profiles with basic information
*MUST-HAVE* - Essential for building your client base and tracking relationships

**Edit Client** - Modify existing client information and details
*MUST-HAVE* - Client information changes over time, requiring updates

**Delete Client** - Remove inactive or incorrect client entries
*NICE-TO-HAVE* - Useful for cleanup but not critical for core operations

**Search Clients** - Quickly find clients by name, company, or email
*MUST-HAVE* - Efficiency is crucial when managing multiple clients

**View Client Profile** - See complete client information in one place
*MUST-HAVE* - Need comprehensive view for communication and invoicing

**Phone Number Field** - Capture and store client contact numbers
*MUST-HAVE* - Essential for direct communication and follow-ups

**Email Address Field** - Store and manage client email contacts
*MUST-HAVE* - Primary communication channel for most freelancers

**Address Field** - Record physical addresses for clients
*NICE-TO-HAVE* - Useful for local clients or physical deliverables

**Company Field** - Store client company names and details
*MUST-HAVE* - Important for professional communication and invoicing

**Notes Field** - Add custom notes about each client
*MUST-HAVE* - Critical for remembering client preferences, history, and special details

## ACTIVITY HISTORY

**Log Call** - Record phone calls with clients
*MUST-HAVE* - Essential for tracking communication and follow-ups

**Log Email** - Document email exchanges with clients
*MUST-HAVE* - Important for communication history and accountability

**Log Meeting** - Record in-person or virtual meetings
*MUST-HAVE* - Critical for project planning and relationship building

**Log Note** - Add general activity notes
*MUST-HAVE* - Flexible way to capture any client interaction

**View Timeline** - See all client activities in chronological order
*MUST-HAVE* - Provides complete picture of client relationship history

**Edit Activity** - Correct or update past activities
*MUST-HAVE* - Mistakes happen, need ability to fix them

**Delete Activity** - Remove incorrect or outdated activities
*NICE-TO-HAVE* - Cleanup tool but not essential for daily operations

## INVOICING

**Create Invoice** - Generate new invoices for clients
*MUST-HAVE* - Core functionality for earning money

**Line Items** - Add multiple services/products with descriptions and prices
*MUST-HAVE* - Required for detailed billing and transparency

**Mark Sent** - Track when invoices are sent to clients
*MUST-HAVE* - Essential for follow-up and cash flow management

**Mark Paid** - Update invoice status to paid
*MUST-HAVE* - Critical for tracking revenue and financial health

**Void Invoice** - Cancel invoices that were sent by mistake
*NICE-TO-HAVE* - Useful for error correction but not always needed

**Print View** - Generate printable invoice formats
*MUST-HAVE* - Some clients prefer physical copies or PDFs

**Due Date** - Set payment deadlines for invoices
*MUST-HAVE* - Essential for cash flow management and reminders

**Overdue Flag** - Automatically flag late payments
*MUST-HAVE* - Critical for collections and financial tracking

## ADDRESS BOOK

**Client Address** - Store complete client mailing addresses
*NICE-TO-HAVE* - Only needed if you have physical deliverables or local clients

**Edit Address** - Update client address information
*NICE-TO-HAVE* - Useful for address changes but not essential for most freelancers
