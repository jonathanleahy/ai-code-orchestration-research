### Internal Linking Strategy
- Link to related blog posts within content (3-5 per article)
- Link to product pages from relevant content pieces
- Create a "Related Resources" section at the end of each post
- Link from homepage to most relevant blog posts
- Cross-link between comparison posts and feature guides

## Technical SEO

### Site Speed Requirements
- Page load time: Under 2 seconds (target 1.5 seconds)
- Mobile page speed: Under 3 seconds (target 2.5 seconds)
- Core Web Vitals: Good (LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1)
- Image optimization: Compressed without quality loss
- Minimize JavaScript and CSS files

### Mobile-First Design
- Responsive design for all screen sizes
- Touch-friendly navigation and buttons
- Optimized font sizes for mobile reading
- Fast loading images with proper sizing
- Mobile navigation menu with clear hierarchy

## Link Building

### 5 Strategies for Building Backlinks

1. **Create High-Value Resource Content**
   - Develop comprehensive guides, templates, and checklists
   - Create downloadable resources that freelancers will want to share
   - Build infographics with actionable insights

2. **Freelancer Community Engagement**
   - Participate in freelancer forums and communities
   - Share valuable insights and solutions in relevant discussions
   - Build relationships with freelancer influencers and thought leaders

3. **Guest Posting and Content Marketing**
   - Contribute to established freelancer and business blogs
   - Share case studies and success stories from real clients
   - Create tutorial content that showcases CRM benefits

4. **Partnership and Collaboration**
   - Partner with complementary software tools
   - Collaborate with freelance coaches and consultants
   - Cross-promote with other freelancer service providers

5. **Local and Industry Directory Listings**
   - Submit to freelance and business directories
   - List in industry-specific platforms and communities
   - Create local business listings for regional freelancers

### Guest Posting Targets

1. **Freelancer.com Blog** - High traffic, relevant audience
2. **The Balance Careers** - Professional development content
3. **Entrepreneur.com** - Business and productivity articles
4. **ProBlogger** - Content marketing and productivity focus
5. **Small Business Trends** - Small business and freelance resources
