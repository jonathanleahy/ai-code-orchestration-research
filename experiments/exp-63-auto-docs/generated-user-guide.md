# CRM Web Application User Guide

Welcome to your new Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system! This guide will help you get started with managing your clients, tracking activities, and handling invoices — all from your web browser.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Adding Your First Client

**What to do:**
1. Open your web browser and go to [http://localhost:8080](http://localhost:8080).
2. On the dashboard page, look for a button or link labeled **"Add New Client"** or similar.
3. Click the button to open the client creation form.

**What to fill in:**
- **Full Name**: Enter the full name of the client (e.g., "John Doe").
- **Email Address**: Enter their email address (e.g., "john@example.com").
- **Phone Number**: Enter their phone number (e.g., "555-0101").

**What happens next:**
- After filling in the details, click **Save** or **Create Client**.
- The new client will appear on your main dashboard list.

---

### 2. Viewing Client Details

**What to do:**
1. On the dashboard page, locate the list of clients.
2. Find the name of the client you want to view.
3. Click on the client’s name or a **"View Details"** button next to their name.

**What happens next:**
- You will be taken to a detailed view page for that client.
- Here you can see their contact information, activity history, and invoices.

---

### 3. Logging Activities (Calls, Emails, Meetings)

**What to do:**
1. Go to the client detail page for the person you want to log an activity for.
2. Look for a section titled **"Activities"** or a button like **"Add Activity"**.
3. Click the **"Add Activity"** button.

**What to fill in:**
- **Activity Type**: Choose from options like “Call”, “Email”, “Meeting”.
- **Date & Time**: Select the date and time of the activity.
- **Description**: Briefly describe what happened during the activity (e.g., “Discussed project timeline”).

**What happens next:**
- Once submitted, the activity will appear in the client’s activity log.
- It helps you keep track of all interactions with each client.

---

### 4. Creating Invoices

**What to do:**
1. Navigate to the client detail page.
2. Look for a section labeled **"Invoices"** or a button like **"Create Invoice"**.
3. Click the **"Create Invoice"** button.

**What to fill in:**
- **Invoice Number**: Enter a unique number for this invoice.
- **Due Date**: Select when the payment is due.
- **Amount**: Enter the total amount owed.
- **Description**: Add a short note about what the invoice covers (e.g., “Website design services”).

**What happens next:**
- The invoice will be saved and shown in the client’s invoice list.
- You can later mark it as paid or send it to the client.

---

### 5. Managing Invoice Status (Send, Pay)

**What to do:**
1. On the client detail page, find the **"Invoices"** section.
2. Locate the invoice you want to update.
3. Click the **"Mark as Paid"** or **"Send Invoice"** button associated with that invoice.

**What happens next:**
- If you click **"Mark as Paid"**, the system will update the invoice status to “Paid”.
- If you click **"Send Invoice"**, it marks the invoice as sent (you can also print or email it separately).

---

### 6. Searching Clients

**What to do:**
1. On the dashboard page, look for a search bar at the top.
2. Type the name, email, or phone number of the client you're looking for.

**What happens next:**
- The list of clients will automatically filter to show only matching results.
- You can then click on the client’s name to view their details.

---

### 7. Editing and Deleting Clients

**What to do:**
1. Go to the client detail page.
2. Look for an **"Edit"** or **"Update Client"** button.
3. Click it to open the edit form.

**What to fill in:**
- Make changes to the client’s name, email, or phone number.
- Click **Save Changes** when done.

**What to do if you want to delete a client:**
1. On the client detail page, look for a **"Delete Client"** button.
2. Click it and confirm the deletion in the pop-up window.

**What happens next:**
- The client’s information will be updated or removed permanently.
- Be cautious when deleting — there's no undo!

---

## Need Help?

If you have any questions or run into issues:
- Check the system status at [http://localhost:8080/health](http://localhost:8080/health)
- Contact your IT support team or system administrator

That’s it! You’re now ready to use your CRM effectively. Happy managing!
