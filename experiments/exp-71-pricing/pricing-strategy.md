# CRM for Freelancers - Pricing Strategy

## Van Westendorp Analysis (Simulated)

**Too Cheap:** $0-$5/month
- Freelancers expect value for money
- Would signal poor quality or incomplete features
- Risk of being perceived as "free" but with hidden costs

**Cheap:** $6-$15/month
- Good entry point for basic needs
- Attractive to budget-conscious freelancers
- May signal limited functionality

**Expensive:** $25-$40/month
- Competitive with professional tools
- Justifies premium features and support
- May be too high for casual freelancers

**Too Expensive:** $45+/month
- Would compete with enterprise solutions
- Overpriced for most freelancer budgets
- Would require significant ROI justification

## 3-Tier Pricing Structure

### **Free Tier**
**Price:** $0/month
**Target:** New freelancers, those testing the waters
**Features:**
- Basic contact management (50 contacts)
- Simple task tracking
- Email integration (basic)
- 1 project management board
- Email templates (5)
- Basic reporting (limited)
- Community support

### **Pro Tier**
**Price:** $19/month or $190/year (10% annual discount)
**Target:** Serious freelancers, small teams
**Features:**
- Unlimited contacts
- Advanced task management (subtasks, dependencies)
- Project management with Gantt charts
- Email integration (advanced)
- 5 project boards
- Custom email templates
- Client portal
- Basic analytics and reporting
- Priority support
- File sharing (1GB)
- API access

### **Business Tier**
**Price:** $49/month or $490/year (10% annual discount)
**Target:** Growing agencies, freelancers with teams
**Features:**
- Everything in Pro
- Unlimited project boards
- Team management (5 users)
- Advanced reporting and dashboards
- Invoicing and payment processing
- Client portal with branding
- Custom workflows
- Advanced integrations
- 10GB file storage
- 24/7 phone support
- White-label options
- Advanced security features

## Feature Gating Strategy

**Upgrade Drivers:**
1. **Contact Limitations** - Free tier caps at 50 contacts
2. **Project Management** - Advanced features only in Pro/Business
3. **Team Collaboration** - Multi-user features in Business tier
4. **Reporting & Analytics** - Advanced dashboards in Pro/Business
5. **File Storage** - Limited storage in Free/Pro, unlimited in Business
6. **Support Level** - Community vs priority vs 24/7 support
7. **Client Portal** - Basic access in Pro, full customization in Business

## Recommended Launch Price

**Launch Strategy:**
- **Pro Tier:** $19/month (vs $20-25 competitor range)
- **Business Tier:** $49/month (vs $40-49 competitor range)
- **Free Tier:** $0/month (to drive adoption)

**Launch Pricing:** $19/month for Pro (15% below average competitor price)

## Free Trial Strategy

**Free Trial Duration:** 14 days

**Trial Features:**
- Full access to Pro tier features
- 100 contacts
- Unlimited projects
- 5GB storage
- All integrations
- Priority support access

**Trial Conversion Strategy:**
1. **Week 1:** Email reminders about trial expiration
2. **Week 2:** Personalized outreach to inactive users
3. **Day 10:** Special offer for upgrading to Pro ($15/month instead of $19)
4. **Day 12:** Reminder about trial ending in 2 days
5. **Day 13:** Final reminder with limited-time discount

**Conversion Rate Goal:** 25% of trial users convert to paid

**Post-Trial Strategy:**
- 10% discount for 3 months for trial users
- Tier upgrade path from Free to Pro (with 20% discount)
- Referral program for existing customers

## Additional Considerations

**Discount Structure:**
- Annual billing: 10% discount
- Quarterly billing: 5% discount
- Student/Non-profit discount: 30% off

**Payment Options:**
- Monthly and annual billing
- Credit card and PayPal
- Invoice payment for business customers

**Value Proposition:**
- "Professional CRM for freelancers who want to grow"
- "No hidden fees, no long-term contracts"
- "Simple pricing that scales with your business"

This pricing strategy balances accessibility for new freelancers with profitability for scaling businesses, while positioning the product competitively against established CRM solutions.
