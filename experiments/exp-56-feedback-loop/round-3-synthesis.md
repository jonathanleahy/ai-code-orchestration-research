# Round 3 Synthesis

### Top 3 Changes for Next Iteration

1. **Add direct invoice creation and sending functionality** — requested by Sarah, Mike, and Lisa, effort medium
   - Enable users to create, customize, and send invoices directly from the platform
   - Include automated invoice generation based on time tracking and project data
   - Support multiple payment methods and invoice templates

2. **Implement time tracking and project management features** — requested by Sarah and Mike, effort medium
   - Allow users to log time spent on specific projects and clients
   - Provide project-based reporting and billing capabilities
   - Integrate time tracking with invoicing workflows

3. **Rebrand and reposition the platform as a business management tool** — requested by Sarah and Mike, effort small
   - Update the UI/UX to better reflect business management capabilities rather than just infrastructure monitoring
   - Create clear value propositions for service-based businesses and agencies
   - Add visual cues that highlight business management features over technical monitoring

### Deferred (nice but not critical)
- Advanced analytics dashboards for financial reporting
- Multi-user role management for team collaboration
- Integration with popular accounting software

### Rejected (not feasible or not aligned)
- Complete overhaul of the monitoring dashboard (would require major architectural changes)
- Adding complex CRM features (outside the scope of current business needs)
