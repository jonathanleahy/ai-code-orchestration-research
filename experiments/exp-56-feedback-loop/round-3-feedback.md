The system seems reliable overall, but I'm concerned about that CDN degradation incident since it could have affected invoice delivery to my freelancers.

I need a "Bulk Invoice Generator" that can automatically create and send invoices to all freelancers based on their completed work, with pre-filled tax information and the ability to export to standard accounting formats. This would save me 10+ hours per month of manual data entry and reporting.
