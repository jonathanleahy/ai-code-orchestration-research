# Round 1 Feedback

## Sarah (freelance designer)

**1. First impression:** This looks like a fancy monitoring dashboard for enterprise systems, not a tool I'd actually use for tracking my freelance design work.

**2. What works for you:** The status indicators and incident timeline are nice for understanding system reliability - I can see this would be useful for a business that depends on uptime.

**3. What's missing that you NEED:** I need something that integrates with my existing tools (QuickBooks, Google Drive, email), has simple client management, can generate invoices with one click, tracks time spent on projects, and shows upcoming deadlines. I don't need complex system monitoring.

**4. What's confusing:** The interface feels overly technical with all the infrastructure jargon. I have no idea how to add clients, create invoices, or track my hourly rates. It's like trying to use a nuclear submarine's control panel to check my bank account.

**5. Would you pay $15/month for this? Why/why not:** No, because it's not solving my actual problems. I'd rather pay $15/month for something like FreshBooks or Wave that actually helps me run my freelance business - invoicing, client management, time tracking, and payment processing. This is just a pretty status page.

**6. ONE feature request (most important):** A simple "Add Client" button that lets me enter their contact info, project details, and automatically generates an invoice with my hourly rate and time tracking. I want to see all my clients in one place with their current project status and outstanding invoices.

## Mike (agency owner)

**1. First impression:**
This status page is impressively detailed for a SaaS tool, but it's overkill for my agency's simple needs - I just want to know if the CRM is working when I need it.

**2. What works for you:**
The 100% uptime stats and incident history are reassuring. I like that they show actual root causes and resolution times - that's helpful for understanding if I should expect downtime during certain hours.

**3. What's missing that you NEED:**
I need team visibility - seeing who else is working on a client account, bulk operations for 200+ clients, and the ability to assign tasks to team members. Also, I need to know if the CRM will be down during our team meetings or when we're trying to send client emails.

**4. What's confusing:**
The "CDN Performance Degradation" incident shows a 100% uptime for the main service, but I don't know if that means the CRM itself is slow or if it's just the CDN for images/assets. I also don't see what happens when there's a real outage - I'd want to know if my team can still access client data or if there's a backup system.

**5. Would you pay $15/month for this? Why/why not:**
No, not at $15/month. I'd pay $150/month if it gave me team features and bulk operations, but this is just a status page. I need actual functionality that helps me manage 200 clients and 10 people, not just know if the service is up.

**6. ONE feature request (most important):**
Bulk client management with team assignment capabilities - I need to be able to select 50 clients at once and assign them to different team members, set status updates, and see all their activity in one view. I also need the ability to export client data in bulk for reporting.

## Lisa (accountant)

**1. First impression (1 sentence)**
The system looks technically solid but I need to see how it actually handles my specific freelancer invoicing and tax reporting workflows.

**2. What works for you?**
The uptime stats are reassuring - I need reliable access to financial data, and the 99.95% website uptime gives me confidence that I won't lose access during critical reporting periods.

**3. What's missing that you NEED?**
I need automated invoice generation from completed projects, tax summary reports by quarter/year, and the ability to track freelancer payment statuses and outstanding balances. The CRM interface doesn't show me what I actually need for accounting purposes.

**4. What's confusing?**
The interface doesn't clearly separate freelancer management from financial tracking. I can see the system is built for CRM, but I need a financial dashboard that shows me: total revenue, tax obligations, freelancer payment history, and overdue invoices - not just contact information.

**5. Would you pay $15/month for this? Why/why not?**
No, not at that price point. I need something that can handle 50 freelancers with automated invoicing and tax reporting features. $15/month is more for basic contact management, not comprehensive financial tracking that I need for my business.

**6. ONE feature request (most important)**
I need a "Financial Summary Dashboard" that automatically generates monthly tax reports showing: total income per freelancer, tax withholdings, payment statuses, and a consolidated view of all outstanding invoices with due dates - all exportable to my accounting software.
