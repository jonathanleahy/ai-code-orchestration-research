# Screen Review v1

## **Review by Maria — Freelance UX Designer**

### **Overall Verdict**: **NEEDS CHANGES**

### **What Works**:
- The **Login Screen** is clean and straightforward, which is essential for quick access.
- The **Dashboard** shows a good overview of activity, which I’d use daily to check invoice status or create new ones.

### **What's Confusing**:
- The **password field** in the login screen is too long and not visually distinct from the email input. It’s hard to tell they’re separate fields at a glance.
- The **Onboarding Wizard** seems like a good idea, but I’m unsure how it integrates with my existing workflow. If I already have a company name and profile set up, why do I need to go through this?

### **What's Missing**:
- A **client portal** or **invoice preview** screen is completely missing. As a designer, I need to send invoices to clients and let them view and pay them — this is a must-have.
- No **template customization** options are visible in the dashboard or any other screen.
- No **mobile app** or responsive design is mentioned — this is a dealbreaker for me.

### **Priority Fix**:
- **Add a client portal screen** where I can preview invoices and share payment links. This is critical for daily use.

## **Review by James — Freelance Web Developer**

### **Overall Verdict**: **UNHAPPY**

### **What Works**:
- The **Login Screen** is functional and minimal, which is good.
- The **Dashboard** includes a “QuickBooks Sync” button, which is a **must-have** for me.

### **What's Confusing**:
- The **Onboarding Wizard** seems to assume I’m new, but I already have a company and setup — why do I need to repeat it?
- There’s no **time tracking integration** or **project management** screen visible. I rely on tools like Toggl or Asana to track hours, so this is a big gap.
- The **invoice creation** screen is not visible in the wireframes, but I need one that allows me to **track time and generate invoices automatically**.

### **What's Missing**:
- A **time tracking screen** or integration with tools like Toggl or Harvest.
- A **project management dashboard** or screen to track multiple clients and their projects.
- No **multi-user access** or **team collaboration features** are shown — this is a dealbreaker for my team.

### **Priority Fix**:
- **Add a time tracking screen and project dashboard** — without these, I can’t automate billing or manage clients efficiently.

## **Review by Lisa — Freelance Content Writer**

### **Overall Verdict**: **HAPPY**

### **What Works**:
- The **Login Screen** is simple and fast, which is important for someone who works on the go.
- The **Dashboard** gives a good overview of invoices and quick actions, which I can use to manage my clients.

### **What's Confusing**:

## **Overall Verdict: NEEDS REVISION**

**Why?**  
- **Maria** and **Lisa** are both **happy** with the **login and dashboard**, but **Maria** needs a **client portal**, and **Lisa** needs **template customization** and **payment reminders**.
- **James** is **unhappy** due to the **missing time tracking** and **project management** features — which are essential for his workflow.
- The **Onboarding Wizard** is too rigid and doesn’t account for **experienced users** like James and Lisa.

### **Required Fixes**:
1. Add a **client portal** screen for Maria.
2. Add a **time tracking and project dashboard** for James.
3. Add **invoice template customization** and **payment reminder features** for Lisa.
4. Make the **Onboarding Wizard** optional or skipable for returning users.
5. Ensure the **mobile experience** is a priority.

This MVP is a good start, but it needs **significant adjustments** to meet the core needs of all three personas.
